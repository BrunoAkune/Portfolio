Retail Database Project

SQL database created from scratch for a theoretical retail store

Project Overview

Collaborated with a team of five to design and build a comprehensive SQL database for a theoretical retail store. The database tracks key business components including inventory, sales, customers, and employees. Developed and implemented queries to retrieve and analyze data, showcasing the database's functionality and effectiveness in supporting business operations.

The Problem

The company is opening a new physical location, and it requires an SQL database to track inventory levels, sales, and shippers. Without a centralized database system, the business would struggle to manage stock across departments, process transactions efficiently, and maintain supplier relationships.

My Role

Led the group and the database schema design and implemented 15+ SQL queries for business analytics. My responsibilities included:

  • Designing the complete database architecture and entity relationships
  • Coordinating team efforts and ensuring project milestones were met
  • Developing complex SQL queries for data retrieval and analysis
  • Testing and validating database functionality across all modules

The Impact

Database successfully manages products across 4 departments, processes daily sales transactions, employers, customer memberships, and tracks shipments from 3 suppliers. This comprehensive solution provides:

  • Real-time inventory visibility across all departments
  • Streamlined sales processing and transaction tracking
  • Centralized customer and employee data management
  • Efficient supplier coordination and shipment monitoring
  • Data-driven insights through custom analytics queries
