Power BI Dashboard

Business Analytics Dashboard

This interactive Power BI dashboard provides comprehensive business analytics, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions in real-time.

The Problem

Business executives needed a centralized view of key performance indicators across multiple departments, but data was scattered across various systems making it difficult to track progress and identify trends quickly.

My Role

Designed and developed an interactive Power BI dashboard integrating multiple data sources, creating dynamic visualizations with drill-down capabilities and real-time KPI tracking for executive decision-making.

The Impact

Delivered a unified analytics platform that reduced reporting time by consolidating metrics from multiple sources, enabling executives to monitor business performance and make informed strategic decisions based on real-time data insights.

Tobacco Infographic

Public Health Data Visualization

A visually compelling infographic transforming complex tobacco-related health statistics into an accessible narrative that engages audiences and communicates critical public health information.

The Problem

Public health organizations needed to communicate the impact of tobacco use to diverse audiences, but traditional statistical reports were dense and difficult for the general public to understand and engage with.

My Role

Designed an infographic that synthesizes tobacco-related health data into clear visual narratives using charts, icons, and strategic information hierarchy to make complex statistics accessible and engaging.

The Impact

Created a compelling visual communication tool that translates complex health statistics into an easily digestible format, improving public understanding of tobacco's health impacts and supporting evidence-based public health campaigns.

GIS Projects

Geographic Information Systems Analysis

Collection of spatial analysis and mapping projects demonstrating proficiency in GIS technologies, cartography, and geospatial data visualization across ecological and public health domains.

The Problem

Organizations across environmental conservation and public health sectors needed to visualize spatial patterns and geographic distributions to support evidence-based decision-making, but lacked clear visual representations of their spatial data.

My Role

Created professional GIS maps using ArcGIS for diverse applications: mapping Blue Jay nesting habitats for ecological research and tracking influenza outbreak spread for epidemiological surveillance.

The Impact

Delivered actionable spatial intelligence through cartographic visualization, enabling stakeholders to identify geographic patterns, allocate resources effectively, and make data-driven decisions in wildlife conservation and disease control.
